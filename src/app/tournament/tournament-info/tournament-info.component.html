REQUIREMENTS TO JOIN

The following are the requirements for players and teams to submit to be allowed to enter our tournament:

  • Teams must consist of 6 players. 5 active players and one substition player.
  • Submit the list of players and team name.
  • Players age must range from 15 and above to join.
  • The teams must have at least one member that is currently studying in AUP.
  • Players must have written consent that they are allowed to attend and participate in our tournament.
  • Players and teams must submit medical clearance sheet to determine that there would be no complications when playing.

TERMS & CONDITIONS

By joining our tournament, you are agreeing to our terms and conditions, policies and rules. If you are the parent or legal guardian of a player under the age of 17 to 15, you are agreeing to the terms on behalf of yourself and your child. The terms and conditions are the following:

  • The players are allowed by their parents or guardians to participate in our tournament.
  • Players must inform the staff if they have health concerns. Players and teams must submit their medical clearance sheet.
  • Players and teams must follow our policies.
  • Players must maintain a grade of B- to be allowed to participate in our tournament.

TOURNAMENT MECHANICS

  • 10 teams will only be included in our tournament. The 10 teams will be divided into 2 groups.
  • Out of those 10 teams, only the top 8 will proceed to our elimination stage.
  • The team seeding will be determined based on team standings or team wins and loses.
  • In the elimination stage, teams will play one game to determine who will advance to the next round.
  • In the semifinal stage, the teams that would advance would once again play one game to determine who will reach the finals.
  • The last 2 teams will face off in a best of 3 series. The first team to win 2 games will be crowned as the Guardian Tournament Champions.

TOURNAMENT POLICY

The following are the tournament policies that players and teams must follow:

  • Any form of trashtalking is not allowed in game.
  • If a player or a team is caught cheating, the team will be immediately disqualified.
  • Harassment of any kind is not tolerated in our tournament. If a player or team is caught doing such actions, the team or the player is immediately banned from participating or watching our tournament.
